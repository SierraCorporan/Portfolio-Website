Clairvoyage

Clairvoyage is a branding project I had the privilege of contributing to, where I focused on developing a cohesive visual identity for the brand. Throughout the project, we explored the brand’s color palette, imagery, and logo design to create a visual direction that aligned with the client’s vision. Clairvoyage is a therapeutic brand centered around a modern and calming aesthetic, with the letters C and V serving as key elements within the logo design. The goal was to create a minimalist identity that felt clean, sophisticated, and approachable while still maintaining an aesthetically pleasing and memorable appearance.
