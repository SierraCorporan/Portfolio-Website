Youth
October 13th, 2021

Height: 14 inches

Width: 11 inches

Material(s): Colored Pencil

Process(es): The lit candle casting its light across her face accentuates the youthful vibrancy and purity of life. The soft, warm glow highlights the smoothness and vitality of her features, symbolizing the fleeting yet powerful nature of youth.

Extinguish
October 15th, 2021

Height: 14 inches

Width: 11 inches

Material(s): Colored pencil

Process(es): The desaturation of colors in the illustration symbolizes the gradual loss of liveliness and vitality. Just as a candle flickers out and its ashes dissolve into the darkness, the fading hues represent the subject's diminishing presence.

Embodiment
January 23rd, 2022

Height: 17 inches

Width: 14 inches

Material(s): Colored Pencil, Graphite, Acrylic on Bristol Paper

Process(es): The duality of this illustration symbolizes the inevitable cycle of life and death, blending the strength and beauty of the living animal with the stark, inevitable reality of mortality. By merging these elements, I sought to convey the impermanence of life, while also emphasizing the coexistence of life and death in every being.

Decay
January 30th,2022

Height: 19 inches

Width: 16 inches

Material(s): Graphite on Bristol Paper

Process(es): "When initially creating this portrait, my intention was simply to capture the face of an older man. However, as I continued to reflect on the piece, I chose to leave it unfinished, allowing the figure to subtly fade into the white space of the background. This technique evokes the sense that he is gradually ceasing to exist, which is precisely the concept I aimed to convey through this illustration."

Tree of Life
March 7th, 2022

Height: 20 inches

Width: 16 inches

Material(s): Acrylic on canvas using gold to accentuate the use of the tree as the main light source of the image.

Process(es): Tree roots leading toward the figure within this portrait signify a return to the soil from where we began life.
