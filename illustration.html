Youth
October 13th, 2021

Height: 14 inches

Width: 11 inches

Extinguish
October 15th, 2021

Height: 14 inches

Width: 11 inches

Material(s): Colored pencil using blank spaces to create shapes of the missing objects and figure.

Process(es): Desaturation of colors symbolizes the fading liveliness. As the candle extinguishes and the ashes fade into the dark backdrop, so does he.
