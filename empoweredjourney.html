Empowered Journey

Role

Research and Creative Design


Description

Empowered Journey is a support initiative dedicated to empowering young teen mothers by providing access to reliable resources, educational content, and a supportive community. The project aims to promote confidence, personal growth, and informed decision-making through accessible information and thoughtful visual communication.


Purpose

Empowered Journey was created to provide support, resources, and encouragement for young teen mothers as they navigate the challenges of motherhood, education, and personal growth. The project focuses on creating accessible and empowering content that helps young mothers feel informed, supported, and connected through their journey.


The problem

Young teen mothers often face challenges such as limited access to resources, social stigma, lack of guidance, and difficulty balancing motherhood with education and everyday responsibilities. Many may feel isolated or overwhelmed while navigating a major life transition without a strong support system. Empowered Journey addresses this gap by providing accessible information, encouragement, and resources designed specifically to support their needs.



Target Audience

The primary target audience for Empowered Journey is young teen mothers who are seeking support, guidance, and resources as they navigate motherhood. The project is designed for young women who may need encouragement, educational information, and access to a supportive community that helps them build confidence, make informed decisions, and continue working toward their personal goals.



Share Your Story

Share Your Story is one of the core segments of the Empowered Journey project. This section focuses on creating a space where women from different backgrounds and experiences can share their personal journeys with motherhood at a young age. By gathering stories from individuals around the world, we aim to represent a diverse range of perspectives, including women who chose to continue their pregnancies as well as those who chose abortion.

Interview

Empowered Journey is built around the belief that every woman deserves support, understanding, and respect regardless of the path she chooses. Many women may feel hesitant or afraid to openly share their experiences due to fear of judgment or stigma. To address this, the platform provides an option for stories to be submitted anonymously, allowing individuals to share their experiences while maintaining their privacy. These stories serve as a source of comfort, connection, and support for others who may be navigating similar challenges. To the left there is a representation of one of our interviews taken and shared across our platforms.

Visual Style

The visual style of Empowered Journey focuses on creating a warm, welcoming, and empowering experience. The design uses approachable imagery, soft yet confident colors, and clear layouts to communicate feelings of trust, support, and growth. The overall aesthetic is designed to feel safe and encouraging.

Debrief

Working on Empowered Journey strengthened my ability to combine design with meaningful research and storytelling. Throughout the project, I researched topics related to teen motherhood to ensure that the information shared was accurate, supportive, and accessible. I also designed visual mockups and social media content that reflected the project's mission of creating a safe, welcoming, and empowering space for young mothers. One of the most valuable lessons from this project was learning how thoughtful design can communicate empathy and build trust. Every design decision—from the visual style to the organization of information—was made with the goal of making users feel supported rather than judged. This experience reinforced the importance of designing with compassion, conducting thorough research, and creating content that is both visually engaging and meaningful. It also strengthened my skills in visual communication, content organization, and designing for a purpose-driven audience.
