BMCCLUBS

Role

UX/UI Designer

Description

BMCClubs is a mobile app designed to help students become more involved in campus life by making it easy to discover clubs, organizations, and student communities. The app provides detailed information about each club, including its purpose, meeting schedule, membership, and upcoming activities, allowing students to explore opportunities that match their interests. In addition to joining existing organizations, students can create their own clubs, encouraging new communities to form around shared passions and fostering a more connected, inclusive campus experience.

Purpose of Mobile App

This app showcases clubs, student activities, and events while encouraging students to feel welcome, meet new people, and explore new experiences on campus.


The problem

Starting college can feel overwhelming, especially for students entering a large campus where they may not know what resources are available. Many students are unaware of the clubs, organizations, and activities they can participate in, making it difficult to build connections and become involved. BMCCLUBS was created to solve this problem by making campus involvement more accessible, organized, and approachable through a digital platform students already use every day.

Target Audience

The primary target audience for this app is incoming freshmen and sophomores at BMCC who are adjusting to a new campus environment and looking for ways to become more involved. New students may feel overwhelmed, unsure of where to find resources, or unaware of the clubs and communities available to them. BMCC is a large campus with many opportunities, but discovering these resources can be challenging when students are unfamiliar with the environment. This app provides an accessible way for students to explore clubs, learn about campus activities, and connect with others from the comfort of their own devices. By making information easily available, students can discover communities that match their interests without feeling pressured or intimidated.

User Tasks

* Discover and learn about existing clubs and organizations
* Connect and communicate with other students
* Join a welcoming and supportive campus community
* Create new clubs based on personal interests
* View ratings and experiences shared by past club members
* Stay updated on club meetings, events, and announcements

Supported Platforms

* iOS / Apple App Store
* Android / Google Play Store

Competition

A similar platform to BMCC Clubs is Instagram, an application focused on social connection and community building. Instagram allows users to share experiences, discover new people, build relationships, and stay connected with communities they are interested in. BMCC Clubs takes inspiration from these social features while focusing specifically on helping students discover campus organizations and create meaningful connections within their school community.

Visual Style

The visual style of BMCC Clubs is designed to feel modern, friendly, and accessible. The interface focuses on clear organization, simple navigation, and an inviting experience that encourages students to explore new opportunities.

Debrief

This project helped me explore the importance of designing digital experiences that solve real-world problems. BMCCLUBS focuses not only on functionality but also on creating an emotional connection by helping students feel welcomed and supported. Through this process, I gained a deeper understanding of user-centered design, information organization, prototyping, and the importance of designing with the user's needs in mind. The project reinforced how thoughtful design can help create stronger communities and improve the overall student experience.
